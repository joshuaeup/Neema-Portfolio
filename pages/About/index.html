My name is Neema Belton and I am a student at the Savannah College of Art and Design. I am pursuing Film and Television with a concentration in screenwriting. I am originally from Connecticut and moved to North Carolina at a young age. I now split my time during the year between attending school in Atlanta, and being in Charlotte. I have always had a passion for storytelling but more specifically, telling stories that I could relate to. In the film industry, we have all gotten accustomed to watching movies where everyone looks the same. However, that is not reality. We live in a world where some of the most beautiful and intriguing stories come from people of all different walks of life who wear Many different Faces. As a storyteller, I want to ensure that none of these stories go unnoticed or unheard.
